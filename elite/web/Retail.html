Retail Industry

The formula for online success is simple: acquire customers and keep them. Customers want an experience that's easy. They're looking for sites that engage them, and make shopping simple but interesting. It's the key to getting the customers you want. That's where we come in.

From innovative eCommerce websites to robust supply chain management systems, Elite offers innovative, customized solutions that sell. We deliver targeted results on critical issues like PCI compliance and stock-out avoidance, and help you leverage industry-leading business intelligence and reporting tools such as MicroStrategy, Cognos, and Business Objects. Our service delivery model and deep domain experience bring a unique level of flexibility, cost-effectiveness and service excellence to every project.

Industry Challenges

Our integrated services across IT enable retailers to transform their businesses, innovate and optimize processes to outperform competition, and ease operations by creating a customer responsive retail chain.

Major challenges faced by retail industry:

  • Retail consolidation
  • Increasing challenges in acquiring and retaining customers
  • Constrained space and global operations
  • Ever-shortening product lifecycles
  • Differentiation created through business process excellence
  • Increasing competition from newer and more aggressive store formats

Our Solutions

Right product at the right place, with the right price and at the right time is the number one business imperative of all consumer products companies. As markets become more complex and consumers become more demanding, marketing consumer products needs to go through a sea change. With this paradigm change, the need of the hour is to bring in processes and systems that are able to handle all the emerging challenges and also exploit market opportunities as they come up. This includes being able to act rapidly on strategic initiatives that are often driven by changing consumer, shopper, customer, supply chain and technology demands and their potential impact on profitability and growth.

Highlights of our services for the retail sector:

  • We ensure enterprise business transformation with integrated services across consulting, application development and maintenance, infrastructure and BPO.
  • We have built expertise in niche areas such as MDM, merchandizing, supply chain management, supplier relationship management and POS, among others.
